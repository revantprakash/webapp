About Us

CampusHaat is an online platform to sell, rent, donate and buy used/new items in IIT Kanpur. You can use our app to sell/rent/donate items like books, cycles, coolers, laptops & accessories and almost anything. You can post unlimited number of ads, absolutely free of cost.
You will find variety of items on our app across various categories at cheaper rates or, free if it's donated :)

Share books, study materials and other useful things to juniors or anyone in need. People sitting in the upcoming placement season and who will be preparing for various competitive exams.

Going out from the campus?? leave your item with us and we'll transfer you money when it gets sold :)

Rent your cycles during events in the campus like Antaragni, Techkriti, Udghosh etc. on your terms and conditions and earn easy money to pay your canteen bill :)
Rent your cooler in summers, if you are not in the campus.
